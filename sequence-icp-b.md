# Outbound Email Sequence: Institutional EAM (ICP B)

**Target Audience:** COO / Head of Operations at an established EAM (50–500+ clients, 5–20+ staff).
**Core Pain Point:** Ops team is the bottleneck for growth; manual reconciliation across 5–15+ custodians takes days and risks errors/audit findings.
**Tone:** Institutional, authoritative, focused on scale, risk mitigation, and operational efficiency. Acknowledging the complexity of their buying committee.
